URL Classification

Enter a URL. It is sent to your local inference API, encoded exactly as in model_hash.py:url_to_seq(), and classified by your trained CNN-LSTM model via model.predict(). The rationale string is generated by the same build_rationale() rule indicators used in your forensic pipeline.

    Tamper-Evidence Lab

    Pick a logged record and edit its data directly — simulating an attacker rewriting evidence after the fact. Re-verify to see exactly where the chain breaks, then try to "cover up" the tamper by re-linking downstream hashes, and check it against an independently-stored verification anchor.
